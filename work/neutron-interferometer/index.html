Holography with a neutron interferometer

(a) The off-axis method of optical holography of semitransparent objects introduced by Leith and Upatnieks. (b) An artistic depiction of the neutron holography experiment.

October 2016 • JQI • https://www.osapublishing.org/oe/abstract.cfm?uri=oe-24-20-22528 • Tagged: Diagrams

Project Notes

This was created as a paper figure for an experiment using a Mach-Zehnder interferometer to perform neutron holography of a spiral phase plate. The figure compares the original optical method of holography to the new method (using neutrons instead of photons).
