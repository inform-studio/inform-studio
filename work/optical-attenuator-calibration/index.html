Measuring optical power with single-photon detectors

Light from a pulsed laser (a) is directed through a variable attenuator (b) onto a transition edge sensor device (c). The resulting signal (d) is analyzed with a statistical clustering algorithm (e) which returns a nominal photon number (f).
